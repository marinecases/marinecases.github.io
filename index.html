DAG Seminar: Tannaka Duality for Geometric Stacks

Seminar Website

This is the website for the DAG seminar centred around Tannaka duality for geometric stacks.

The goal of the seminar is to understand Stefanich's Tannaka duality statement, which refines Lurie's result by eliminating the tameness hypotheses, by means of an affineness result.

Prerequisites for the seminar are a bare-bones knowledge of derived algebraic geometry and an understanding of infinity-categories.
