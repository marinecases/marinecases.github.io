Quantum Geometric Langlands Seminar

Seminar Website

This is the website for the Quantum Geometric Langlands Seminar.

The goal for the seminar is to set up the quantum geometric langlands conjecture and its desired global-to-local properties, and prove as much as possible in the case of G=T. It runs in parallel to the online derived algebraic geometry seminar :discordinvite
